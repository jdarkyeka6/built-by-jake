Our story

A bakery built on early mornings.

Crumb & Co. started with one home oven, a sourdough starter named Doughlores, and a stubborn belief that good bread takes time.

Years later we're still small on purpose. Everything is mixed, shaped and baked by hand — no shortcuts, no improvers, nothing you couldn't pronounce. Just flour, water, salt, butter and patience.

Walk in before 7am and the place still smells like the very first loaf.

— the Crumb & Co. bakers
